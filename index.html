About Me

Hello, my name is Matthew Belanic. I am from Tustin, California. From 2010 to 2014, I attended St. Mary's College of California in Moraga, where I earned a B.A. in History. During my time at St. Mary's, I took a Python coding course, and found a new passion in programming. Upon graduation, I entered an online master's program for software engineering at California State University: Fullerton. I completed the program in May 2016, and shortly after began interning at Golden Communications, a web development company specializing in eCommerce, in Costa Mesa. I am currently interning here as a back-end developer. Aside from Python, I have had limited experience programming in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, and SQL. I have also done work in Quality Assurance while interning at Golden Communications.
